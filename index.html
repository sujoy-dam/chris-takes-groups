The Philippines is more than just El Nido and Coron. This epic 10-day group trip takes you island hopping through the Visayas (including Siquijor), a region famous for its natural wonders, picturesque beaches, friendly locals, and world-class marine life.

Trip highlights

Boat trip to Apo Island

Snorkel and dive at Apo Island, a marine paradise teeming with coral reefs and sea turtles.

Bohol countryside tuk-tuk tour

Visit the famous Chocolate Hills, Tarsier Sanctuary and a hidden paradise for an 8-course farm-to-table lunch.

Sardine run

Swim with millions of sardines – a world-famous natural phenomenon.

Canyoneering at Kawasan Falls

Slide, climb, jump and get the adrenaline-pumping at Kawasan Falls.
